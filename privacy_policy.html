Privacy Policy – IELTS SpeakPro AI

Last updated: July 22, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The app records your voice responses for IELTS speaking practice. However, we do not store any audio recordings. They are used temporarily for real-time evaluation only.

2. Use of OpenAI API

Your spoken responses are securely sent to OpenAI’s API for feedback generation. We do not store or reuse your data. You can review OpenAI's privacy policy at this link.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except for temporary processing by OpenAI.

4. Data Retention

No audio data or user inputs are stored. All processing is temporary and discarded after use.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
